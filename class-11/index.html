BASIS Monthly
Magazine "Smart Bangladesh"

Being inspired by the Vision 2041, transforming Digital Bangladesh to SMART Bangladesh by 2041, BASIS has launched it's first ever magazine 'SMART Bangladesh' -- the premier ICT industry voice of the country, on 12 November 2022.

mission & vission

mission

To develop the ambition, capacity and sustainable growth of the BASIS member companies, and to lead and deliver the BASIS contribution to Smart Bangladesh.

vission

o lead the development of a vibrant, sustainable and successful software and IT service industry in Bangladesh.
